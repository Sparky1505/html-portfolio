Once you buy the components above mentioned, you are ready to build your own PC.

Steps breakdown

Now Let's look at at the steps how we can start building the PC

Step 1 - Installing CPU on Motherboard

  1. UnWrap the Motherboard and gently place it on top of the Motherboard box
  2. On top of CPU slot you will see a plastic cover, do not worry about it now. Unclip the bracket and leave it for now
  3. Unbox the CPU, this is very important part, make sure not to touch the pin in CPU
  4. Once you carefully took the CPU out of the box, make sure to clearly check the CPU, you will see a white triangle on one of the corner
  5. You need to align with the exact same triangle in the Motherboard CPU slot
  6. Once you see it, place the CPU gently and DO NOT press or give any pressure
  7. Once the CPU is in place, close the CPU bracket, it will automatically pop the plastic cover

Now you have successfully completed the step 1 and installed the CPU on the Motherboard. Let's continue
